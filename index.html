Label definitions

SAFE
Normal call, no social-engineering risk. No pressure, no sensitive requests.
SUSPICIOUS
Contains pressure, urgency, authority impersonation, or unusual instructions — but does NOT directly request credentials, money, or remote access.
LIKELY_VISHING
Asks for sensitive credentials (OTP, password, PIN, card details), remote access (AnyDesk, TeamViewer), money transfer, crypto, gift cards, or demands secrecy.
